What is Study Buddy?

Study Buddy is a resource for studying in any area. You can create or join study groups for any class or subject where you can study as a group or get help.

How to Access Study Buddy

If you already have an account go ahead and log in and once you do, it will take you directly to the home page where you will now see more options in the navigation bar. If you do not have an account, click log in and create an account using your email and once verified, you will be able to access study groups.
